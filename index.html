I am currently a Nanyang Assistant Professor starting from Fall 2024. My research interests include data security and privacy, database theory and algorithms, and machine learning. Previously, I was a postdoctoral fellow at Carnegie Mellon University co-supervised by Prof. Elaine Shi and Prof. Giulia Fanti. Before that, I received my PhD at the CSE department of HKUST, where I was very fortunate to work with Prof. Ke Yi.
I am actively looking for highly motivated Ph.D. students and Visiting Interns to join the group. If you are interested, please send me an email by attaching your CV!
